My name is Dalton Wood. I am a student currently pursuing a bachelor's degree at Western Governors University. I'm a veteran, but as challenging and rewarding as my military career was, it was a far cry from anything IT related. After years of honorable service, I moved on to face new challenges as a civilian. I completed a technical certification program, and was able to launch my career in IT. I have realized over the last few years just how broad and dynamic the field of Information Technology is, and it's been a battle finding my place.

In 2024, I decided to pursue a degree in Software Engineering with no prior experience. Again, I learned just how broad even a subfield of IT can be - a myriad of languages, development methodologies, complex structures and algorithms, and design philosophies. At this point in my journey, I am just scratching the surface of so many different areas. I enjoy the aesthetic and organizational aspects of front-end development, but I continue to challenge myself across all aspects of software. Below, you will find a few projects that I hope will show you how much I've learned in a short time.

As a junior at WGU, I am actively seeking internships, volunteer work, and other opportunities to provide real-world value with these new skills. If you would like to connect or provide feedback of any kind, please check out my projects and fill out the contact form at the bottom of the page.

Thanks for stopping by.
